Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Dosely Pro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information.

1. Data Collection

Dosely Pro does not collect personal data from users.

All information such as medications, reminders, and profiles is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions only to enable core features:

  • Notifications: Used to send medication reminders.
  • Photos: Used if you choose a profile picture.
  • Camera: Used if you take a profile photo.

These permissions are optional and can be disabled anytime from system settings.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure system storage. We do not have access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

Dosely Pro does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Dosely Pro does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for family use.

7. Security

We rely on your device's operating system security and sandbox protection to keep your information safe.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
